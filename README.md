# Enterprise Risk Assessment & Risk Register Program

## Overview

This repository contains a reusable **Enterprise Risk Assessment & Risk Register Program** designed to identify, score, prioritize, and govern security risks across enterprise technology environments.

The program enables leadership to make informed risk treatment and acceptance decisions while ensuring risks are consistently documented, escalated, and reviewed over time.

---

## Program Objectives

- Establish a standardized enterprise risk methodology
- Identify and prioritize security risks across domains
- Apply consistent likelihood and impact scoring
- Define clear escalation and treatment rules
- Integrate risks with existing governance programs

---

## Risk Domains Covered

- Identity & Access Management
- Third-Party / Vendor Risk
- Zero Trust & Remote Access
- SOX IT General Controls
- Cloud & Infrastructure
- Monitoring & Incident Response

---

## How to Navigate This Repo

1. Start with governance documents in `docs/`
2. Review scoring and escalation in `registers/`
3. Explore example enterprise risks in `scenarios/`
4. See how risks integrate with other programs in `integration/`
5. Review evidence handling expectations in `evidence/`

This repository is a portfolio demonstration of an enterprise risk governance capability. All examples are simulated. Do not upload real organizational risk data, credentials, or sensitive artifacts.
