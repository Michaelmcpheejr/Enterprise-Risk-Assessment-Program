## Risk Statement

If high-risk third-party SaaS vendors are not consistently assessed and monitored, then vendor security weaknesses could lead to data exposure or regulatory violations due to insufficient third-party governance.

## Treatment

Mitigate through vendor risk assessments and continuous monitoring, and transfer risk through contractual obligations and insurance where applicable.
