## Risk Statement

If user and privileged access to SOX-relevant systems is not periodically reviewed and remediated, then inappropriate access could persist, resulting in unauthorized financial activity or audit deficiencies.

## Treatment

Mitigate through formal access review procedures, defined review cadence, and remediation tracking with audit evidence.

## Review Cadence

Quarterly
