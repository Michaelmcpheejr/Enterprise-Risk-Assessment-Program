## Purpose

Establish a standardized enterprise risk assessment framework to identify, score, prioritize, and govern security risks, enabling leadership to make informed risk treatment and acceptance decisions.

## Scope

This program applies to enterprise technology risks including identity, vendors, cloud platforms, applications, data protection, monitoring, and incident response readiness.

## Ownership Model

- GRC owns the risk methodology and facilitation
- Control owners manage remediation
- Leadership reviews and accepts residual risk
