# Executive Risk Summary

## Top Enterprise Risks

- Identity & Privileged Access Misuse
- Third-Party Vendor Security Failures
- Zero Trust Remote Access Bypass
- SOX ITGC Access Control Failures

## Risk Trends

- Identity and vendor risks remain primary focus areas
- Monitoring and cloud risks are stable but require maturity improvements

## Executive Actions Required

- Continued investment in identity governance
- Formal oversight of high-risk vendors
- Enforcement of Zero Trust access paths
